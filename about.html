Who am I?

I am an aspiring UX engineer, a fashion enthusiast, an artist, and a singer with a vision of the world where technology and art can meet seamlessly.

I have great programming skills, but I also have a keen eye for aesthetics.

Background

I was born and raised in Busan, South Korea, a beautiful city bordering the Pacific Ocean.

​Busan is a great city to live, but I have always aspired to explore more of the world since I was very young.

College

I came to Medford, MA in 2018. I am currently a sophomore at Tufts University majoring in Computer Science and Engineering Psychology and minoring in Studio Art.

Interests

I am particularly interested in the field of Human-Computer Interaction, and I develop both technological and artistic solutions to approach problems that emerge from digital interactions.

In my free time, I usually sing, hike, dance, go travel, and meet new people. I enjoy exploring and learning about new things everyday.
