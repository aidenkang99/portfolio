UI/UX Designer

I created a high-fidelity prototype of a mobile application that allows users to search cool outfits based on machine learning.

Users can swipe each outfit based on their preferences, and the system uses machine learning to suggest outfits that would fit the user's style much more accurately.

Users can also type certain keywords to look for outfits that they are looking for. The app also automatically creates a "lookbook" that contains pictures of their favorite outfits.

UX Research Process

I have conducted UX research methods such as persona modeling, empathy map, and concept model process to examine potential users’ wants and needs.

This process helped me how to organize information in my application in a way that would be intuitive to the users. Especially, because most similar applications in the fashion industry do not have the “lookbook” functionality, I tried to gain more insight about the “lookbook” page through this research process.

Wireframing

Based on the insight from Persona Modeling, I added a functionality to my wireframe so that the user could create a lookbook by simply indicating their interests through swiping pictures.

Prototyping

By using Adobe XD and Illustrator, I created an interactive prototype of the application. Viewers can swipe the pictures to indicate their interest and look at a mockup of a lookbook that will be created based on past records. Viewers can also search looks by keywords to discover new styles.
